Welcome

Why Enroll:

Every lebanese is allowed to join the party. Regardless of one's ideology. You will have the right to argue, propose laws, vote, and be part of the democratic process in the LWP. Without every capable individual enrolling, we will not reach the goal. If you're persuaded by the party's main goals, please enroll. We need you. We need everyone.

To Enroll:

  1. Prepare your National Identity (الهويه)
  2. Go to the nearest location from below (contact them and they will help you)
  3. Sign a paper that confirms your enrollment in the party and fill other info
  4. After a couple of days, you'll be sent 'Membership ID' that allows you to login. The ID will be sent through Email you provided
